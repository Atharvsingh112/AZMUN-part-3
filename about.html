About AZMUN

AZMUN (Ayoniz Model United Nations) is an international simulation of the United Nations where students engage in global diplomacy, policy-making, and public speaking.

Our Mission

To create a platform for young leaders to develop diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and a global perspective on pressing international issues.

Our Vision

To empower the next generation of changemakers through debate, collaboration, and impactful discussions.
